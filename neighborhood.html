To use Neighborhood Mapper, click on the Pencil and click to start drawing a box around your area of interest, then the Check when you're done.

If you want to start over, click the Redo icon.

To download your data, click the Download button.
Your download area is too large or has too many businesses. Please try again with a smaller area.
You must pick at least 3 points for your download area.
Click Export to download your map, then put the data.geojson file in the "Data" folder of neighborhoodmap.
